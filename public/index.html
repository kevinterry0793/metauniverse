The Ecosystem

We founded RedFOX Labs in 2018 with the intent to be Southeast Asia's first blockchain & digital venture builder and to help with the mass adoption of crypto and blockchain along with other new and emerging technology.


We identify and replicate successful pure-play business models for the local markets within the fastest growing sectors across several industries.


Our focus is to unlock the true market potential of the digital economy for high demand products and services such as e-Commerce, e-Media (online video streaming services), Esports and gaming, e-Travel, and ride-hailing/logistics.


Through our ever-growing and advancing ecosystem for digital economy development, we are becoming one of the top, go-to decentralized mobile applications (dApp) in Southeast Asia.
