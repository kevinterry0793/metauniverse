About Doont rug

PRIVACY PROTECTION

Do you know that your transaction records and balances can be traced with most of all the cryptocurrencies?
Doont rug is based on a theory, called zero-knowledge proofs, to protect your privacy with shielded addresses.
Of course, you can secure open transactions to use transparent addresses, as necessary.

Doont rug is open source and we keep discussing and developing Doont rug actively in the open community.
Is Doont rug someone's? Absolutely no. Doont rug is always everyone's.
We are looking forward to having you join us.
